## Design Principles

1. **Warm palette** -- All neutrals are warm-toned (hue range 50-80), never cool/blue grays.
2. **Orange as accent** -- Orange is reserved for CTAs, interactive highlights, and key brand moments. It should pop against the warm charcoal and off-white backgrounds.
3. **High contrast** -- The accent orange (`#B55000`) is chosen to maintain 4.5:1 contrast ratio against white for accessibility (WCAG AA).
4. **Minimal & professional** -- Clean layouts, generous whitespace, subtle warm borders.
5. **Bilingual** -- The site supports English (LTR) and Hebrew (RTL). Logos and assets should work in both directions.

## Logo Guidelines

When designing the logo:

- **Primary colors to use**: Accent Orange (`#B55000` or `#E87F25`) and Primary Charcoal (`#2D1D14`)
- **On dark backgrounds**: Use Accent Bright (`#FB8304`) and Primary Foreground (`#F9F4EE`)
- **Monochrome version**: Use Primary Charcoal (`#2D1D14`) on light, Primary Foreground (`#F9F4EE`) on dark
- **Minimum clear space**: Maintain padding equal to the height of the logo mark on all sides
- **File formats needed**: SVG (for web), PNG with transparency (for docs/social), and favicon (16x16, 32x32, 180x180)
